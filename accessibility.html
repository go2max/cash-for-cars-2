Accessibility

Accessibility statement

We want the website and vehicle-offer process to be usable by as many people as possible.

Our approach

We work to provide keyboard navigation, readable contrast, clear labels, meaningful headings, visible focus states, responsive layouts, text alternatives, and understandable form feedback.

Need assistance?

If a page or form is difficult to use, call 279-675-1575 or email Support@NorCalCashForCars.com. Please describe the page, the problem, and your preferred contact method.

Feedback

Accessibility feedback is reviewed as part of ongoing site maintenance. We will make reasonable efforts to provide the requested information or service through an accessible alternative.

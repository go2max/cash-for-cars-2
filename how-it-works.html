How it works

A clear process from offer to pickup.

Know what information to prepare, what happens during pickup, and when payment takes place.

01

Tell us about the vehicle

Provide your contact information, ZIP code, vehicle type, paperwork status, key status, and any useful condition details.

Start the request
02

Discuss the offer

We may ask for the year, make, model, mileage, condition, title status, and pickup location before confirming the next step.

03

Choose a pickup time

If you accept, we coordinate a location and time. Tow-truck access and local availability can affect scheduling.

04

Complete paperwork and payment

The legal owner or authorized representative completes the required documents before the vehicle is released and payment is completed.

Prepare for pickup

Remove personal items and gather what you have.

  • Vehicle title or other ownership documents
  • Valid identification
  • Keys, if available
  • Registration, if available
  • Any lender or lien information

Ready to request your offer?

There is no obligation to sell after submitting the request.

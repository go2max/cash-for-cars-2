Website purpose

This website allows visitors to request information about a possible vehicle purchase. Submitting a form does not require you to sell and does not require NorCal Cash for Cars to purchase the vehicle.

Vehicle information

You agree to provide accurate information about ownership, condition, location, keys, liens, and paperwork. Final eligibility, pickup, and payment may depend on inspection and verification of the vehicle and ownership documents.

Ownership and authorization

You must be the legal owner or have valid legal authority to sell the vehicle. Additional documentation may be required for co-owned vehicles, estates, businesses, liens, or authorized representatives.

Offers and scheduling

Website requests and preliminary discussions are not final purchase agreements. Pickup timing and availability depend on location, access, vehicle condition, documentation, and scheduling.

Acceptable use

Do not misuse the website, submit false or unlawful information, attempt unauthorized access, interfere with service, or use automated systems to abuse the offer form.

Website availability

We may update, suspend, or discontinue website features. To confirm current information or report a problem, contact Support@NorCalCashForCars.com.
